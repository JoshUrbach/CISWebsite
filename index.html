Xiaomi Cyberdog: Our Newest Addition to the AI Lab

April 27th, 2022

We will be gaining a Xiaomi Cyberdog for the AI Lab that will allow us to get hands-on experience with an artificial intelligence robot. This robot will be a a great addition to the growing AI/ML specialization offered at Gwynedd Mercy, and is said to act pet-like. CyberDog is said to be able to move at a top speed of 3.2m/s, carry a payload of up to 3kg, and is able to perform backflips.

Adjunct Professor of the Year: Professor Knorr!

April 26th, 2022

Professor Knorr was the receipient of the 2022 School of Arts and Sciences Award for Excellence in Adjunct Teaching! He has been with Gwynedd Mercy University's Computer Information Science team for 10 years, and expressed how meaningful it was to be honored by his students when given the award. He is the first adjunct professor in the CIS department to receive this award, and was up against 77 other eligible instructors. The award is based on a survey that goes to all undergraduate students, and this year there was a very high response rate.

Congratulations, Professor Knorr!
